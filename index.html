Helmi Youth Ministry

For ages 16-25

Every Friday at 19:00

What is Helmi?

Helmi is our vibrant youth ministry for young people aged 16-25. Led by Pastor Cleon Alleyne, we meet every Friday evening!

What We Do:

  • Worship and praise
  • Bible study
  • Games and fellowship
  • Food and refreshments
  • Regular camps and activities

Support & Resources

Struggling with Depression?

You are not alone. God loves you and we are here to support you.

  • Weekly prayer support - Thursdays 18:00
  • Pastoral counseling available
  • "The Lord is close to the brokenhearted" - Psalm 34:18

Overcoming Addiction?

There is hope and freedom in Christ.

  • Confidential support meetings
  • Prayer partners available
  • "If the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed" - John 8:36
